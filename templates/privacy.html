Privacy Policy

Last Modified: May 2026

1. Information We Collect

To deliver high-performance cross-border transaction services safely, Adigini Remit collects specific personal identifiers when processing operations:

  • Sender Information: Legal name, phone record routing targets, and financial transaction history credentials.
  • Recipient Information: Target payout names, mobile money accounting data records, and localized banking distribution details.
  • Technical Footprints: Session metadata records, cookies, and IP authentication tracking flags.

2. How We Utilize Data

Your collected parameter vectors are strictly utilized to execute core platform operations:

  • Initializing secure payment frameworks through our third-party infrastructure partner (Paystack).
  • Routing dynamic administrative alerts regarding critical transactional pipeline updates.
  • Enforcing active KYC validation and anti-fraud monitoring layers.

3. Third-Party Data Disclosures

We do not broker, trade, or distribute user identification matrices to external marketing syndicates. Data components are exclusively routed to regulatory authorization agencies and payment gateway clearing instances (Paystack API networks) to guarantee settlement validation protocols.

4. Data Protection Architecture

Adigini Remit uses production-grade security infrastructure. Your information is isolated behind continuous SSL encryption layers, secure multi-factor authentication challenges, and local hardware session flushing calls when running platform executions.
